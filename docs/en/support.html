Support

Help, requirements and common questions

Documentation in the app

The most complete reference is inside the app: Help → Documentation (window with sections on projects, QLab, OSC, subtitles, translation, audio and video).

Requirements

  • macOS (minimum version as shown on the Mac App Store).
  • A local network so clients (browsers on other devices) can reach the HTTP/WebSocket servers.
  • Some features (e.g. translation) may require internet on the Mac running the app.
  • QLab (Figure 53) when you want to align folders and workflow with a .qlab4 / .qlab5 project.

Subscription

Full use of Accesible requires a subscription managed by Apple (StoreKit). Purchase, renewal and cancellation are handled through your Apple account; the app checks subscription status on the device.

Common issues

  • Audio/video servers won’t start: make sure a project is open or created and that project folders are accessible (including macOS sandbox permissions).
  • Clients can’t see the page: same Wi‑Fi/Ethernet network; use the IP shown by the app, not localhost from another device.
  • OSC not arriving: correct UDP port (e.g. 3333 depending on the feature) and system firewall.
