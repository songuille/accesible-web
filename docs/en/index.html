Live accessibility, integrated with QLab

Accesible is a macOS app for accessible live performance. It works alongside QLab (Figure 53) and offers subtitle servers, translation, audio description and sign language video on your local network.

Download on the Mac App Store Support

The store link will be updated when the app is published.

What’s included

📝

Subtitles

OSC messages and a web page to show text in real time, with styles and advanced commands.

🌐

Translation

Same flow as subtitles; translation to your chosen output language and display on another port.

🎵

Audio description

Playback of pre-recorded audio from your project folders, controlled via OSC.

🎬

Sign language video

MP4 sign language video streaming, sync and OSC controls for the browser client.

Projects and QLab

You can pick your QLab project folder or manage projects in a base location. The app keeps a clear structure for media and configuration.
