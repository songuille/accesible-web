Stage With Me

Line rehearsal app for actors and students on iPhone, iPad, and Android.

What it does

Stage With Me helps actors learn new texts and rehearse with confidence. The app gives line prompts using synthetic voice, records your own voice during practice, and lets you listen back to your delivery.

Key features

  • Synthetic voice prompts to deliver line cues and support self-rehearsal.
  • Voice recording during rehearsal sessions, so you can review your interpretation.
  • Multiple texts and multiple saved sessions per text to track your progress over time.
  • Share your recordings with your director or scene partners (messages, AirDrop, or other apps).
  • Language selection available in Spanish, French, English, Italian, German, Galician, Basque, and Catalan.
