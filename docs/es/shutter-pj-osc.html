Shutter PJ-OSC (macOS)

Controla shutter y power de proyectores PJLink con botones o mediante OSC.

Apps

  • Trigger open/close shutter and on/off power commands.
  • Select one or multiple projectors in the same action.
  • Receive OSC from QLab, Resolume or any OSC sender.
  • Designed to automate projection flow in live shows.
